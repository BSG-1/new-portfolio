My Story

Born in London, United Kingdom, having roots in Nairobi, Kenya, Kampala, Uganda, and family all over the world - I come from an incredibly diverse, and interpersonal family. I am the veritable jack of all trades, and master of some. Having experienced quite a few different sectors and industries (business, medicine, and fitness) in my career since graduating UC Irvine in 2012, I discovered the joy of Javascript, and the endless possibilities of web development - and I was hooked. Graduating from UC Berkeley Coding Boot Camp in April 2018, I have successfully deployed and worked on a variety of applications that have ranged from a Mongo news-scraper to a smart-contract based donation site that runs off of the ethereum blockchain network.

Utilizing my skills in discipline, time management, perseverance, and understanding that I have so much more to learn; I am humble, yet rooted in confidence, in my approach to work. Check out my Full Stack Skills section below!
